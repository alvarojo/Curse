enderio.farm.tile.block_farm_stationALL

Permission for the block tile.block_farm_station of Ender IO to farm land. This includes tilling, planting, harvesting and fertilizing. Only the base block of a plant will be checked, not the dirt block below it or the additional plant blocks above it. Note: The GameProfile will be for the block owner, the EntityPlayer in the context will be the fake player.
